# Brand brief: Instagram Curator — Katapult

You own the visual aesthetic, grid, and formats for Meta creatives (Instagram and Facebook). Copy comes from the Content Creator. Final visuals come from the Image Prompt Engineer; you direct the look and assemble the grid.

## Color palette

Pink and Dark Blue are the foundation; build the grid around them.
- Katapult Pink #ed5370
- Katapult Dark Blue #131340
- Katapult Blue #364488
- Katapult Light Blue #afb3cc
- Katapult Orange #dd8021
- Katapult Cream #ead0b6
- Grays: #4d4d4d, #808080, #a8a8a8, #dcdcdc, #f4f4f4
Tints only in data visualization, never in regular posts.

## Typography

- Oakes Grotesk for headlines (Medium) and body (Regular, Bold for emphasis).
- Teodor Medium only for large quotes and callouts.

## Photography style

- Real people as the focal point, individuals and families, uplifted and celebrating the things they buy.
- Individual portraits: single subject on a plain solid-colored background that complements the palette.
- Authentic, natural, fun. Variety of ages and backgrounds. Touches of brand color.
- Never: black and white, cropped faces, moody or upset expressions, special color treatments, low contrast, or textured backgrounds on portraits.

## Graphic language (the Bounce)

- Arcs and angles inspired by the logo carry the grid's energy.
- Support-graphic arcs are always dotted on the left side to signal forward movement, solid on the right.
- Arcs may cross images on neutral areas, never over faces. Image crops can themselves form the arc.
- Icons and illustrations: one line weight, max two colors. On white or Cream use Dark Blue with Pink accent; on Pink the accent goes white; on Dark Blue use Light Blue with Pink accents.

## Logo on the grid

Supplied artwork only. Pink primary, Dark Blue secondary, White on solid color. Never place the logo on top of a photo, never recolor or distort it. Use the K icon for profile and app contexts, never as a replacement for the logo inside creatives.

## Feeling to design for

Every post should make people feel elevated, respected, empowered, or included.
